Cindy Deuser

Director of Human Resources

Cindy is the Human Resource Director and “all things people” at ClickSWITCH. She is the HR business partner supporting all business units in the areas of employee engagement, talent management, performance management, recruiting, benefits, compensation, office management and growing a winning culture.

This is Cindy’s fifth tech start-up in the Twin Cities that she has been a part of. She also worked in Human Resources at Target Corporation and went back a second time to play a major role in Visual Merchandising within the Target stores. One of Cindy’s top highlights in her career was building her own company of fashion boutiques and growing the franchise to over forty stores in eight different States.
