Jane Patterson

Director of Strategic Relationships

Jane Patterson is the Director of Strategic Relationships at ClickSWITCH. She is responsible for building and managing strategic relationships with our Tier One customers while helping them identify opportunities for growth and implementing best practices that support their overall company objectives.

Jane is a highly motivated professional with over 35 years of experience working in roles which have utilized her talents and skills in managing all aspects of relationship management and business development.

Jane is an active volunteer for the Angel Foundation which is a Minnesota based organization that provides emergency financial assistance, education and support to local adults with cancer and their families.
