Awards


20 Fintech Companies to Watch in 2017

ClickSWITCH has been honored as one of Fintech Forward Companies to Watch by BAI and American Banker. ClickSWITCH was recognized for its revolutionary automated switch solution and impressive adoption by financial institutions. View the full list of 20 Fintech Companies to Watch for 2017.

ClickSWITCH Selected as Minnesota Business Magazine's Top 100 Companies to Work for in 2018

ClickSWITCH has been recognized as one of Minnesota Business Magazine's Top 100 Companies to Work for in 2018. This award recognizes Minnesota's top companies.

Winner of the 2018 Benzinga Fintech Innovation Challenge

ClickSWITCH attended the Benzinga Fintech Summit on November 14th, 2018 in San Francisco. The company competed against five innovative Fintech companies presenting a two-minute lightning pitch on the main stage to a full audience and a panel of judges. ClickSWITCH won the competition.

2019 Inno On Fire Winner

ClickSWITCH has been chosen as a 2019 Minne Inno - Inno On Fire Winner. Minne Inno’s annual awards honor Minnesota’s top startups and entrepreneurs.

Minne Inno's 2018 Coolest Companies - Audience Choice Winner

We asked our readers to tell us about the most interesting, innovative startups in Minnesota. We received dozens upon dozens of nominations, and ultimately ended up with a finalist pool of nearly 90 companies.

ClickSWITCH Selected to Present at the 2015 BAI Retail Delivery Innovation Showcase

ClickSWITCH was selected as one of 20 financial service innovators to present at the 2015 BAI Retail Delivery Innovation Showcase in Las Vegas, October 14, 2015. View the demo by visiting this link.

ClickSWITCH Finishes 2nd Place to Apple Pay at the CUTECH 2014 Technology Research Consortium Fall Forum

ClickSWITCH participated in the CUTech Fall Forum on October 14-17 at the Silverado Resort in Napa, California. ClickSWITCH presented their technology to a large number of credit unions in attendance, competing against a group of impressive new technologies. ClickSWITCH ranked second only to Apple Pay as the Best Emerging Technology at the consortium.

ClickSWITCH Ranked 2nd at the 2014 CUNA Technology Council Best in Show Speed Round Competition

ClickSWITCH attended the CUNA Technology Council and the Operations, Sales and Service Council joint conference on September 21-24 in Las Vegas, Nevada at the Mandalay Bay Resort and Casino.

ClickSWITCH placed 2nd to MX/Money Desktop acquired by Fiserv in the Best of Show Competition following 25 technology speed rounds. Over 500 credit union representatives were in attendance.
